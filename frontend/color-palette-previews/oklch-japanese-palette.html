Subtle, sophisticated colors inspired by traditional Japanese aesthetics. Embodying wabi-sabi (侘寂) - the beauty of imperfection and simplicity.

Japanese Design Philosophy

Wabi-sabi (侘寂) Beauty in simplicity, imperfection, and transience
Shibui (渋い) Subtle, understated elegance without ostentation
Ma (間) Negative space, breathing room, minimalism
Iki (粋) Sophisticated simplicity, refined taste

Warm Neutral Scale

🌸 Recommendation for Your Educational Platform

  • Best for Learning & Growth: Matcha (抹茶) - Natural, calming, perfect for educational context
  • Best for Elegance & Creativity: Sakura (桜) - Unique, sophisticated, memorable
  • Best for Traditional Professional: Ai (藍) - Cultural depth with professionalism
  • Best for Ultra-Minimal: Sumi (墨) - Zen-like simplicity, most subtle

My top recommendation: Matcha (抹茶) for its perfect balance of distinctiveness, professionalism, and alignment with learning/growth themes. The muted green is sophisticated yet approachable, and less common in tech applications.
