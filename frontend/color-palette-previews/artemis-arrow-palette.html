🎯 Recommendation for Artemis/Arrow

Best Overall: Hunter Green

Perfect balance of Artemis (nature/wilderness) and Arrow (growth/progress). Distinctive, professional, and perfectly aligned with educational themes.

Most Thematic: Lunar Silver-Blue

Captures both Artemis's lunar connection and Arrow's precision. Ultra-sophisticated and aspirational.

Most Premium: Celestial Purple

Divine wisdom meets high ambition. Unique and memorable in educational space.

Most Minimal: Moonstone White

Pure focus and clarity. For brands that want ultimate subtlety and precision.

My top recommendation: Hunter Green 🌲
For an educational platform named Artemis/Arrow, Hunter Green is the strongest choice. It perfectly embodies the wilderness/nature aspect of Artemis while representing organic growth and forward progress (arrow). It's distinctive in the tech/education space, professional yet approachable, and timeless.
