### Employer side

| Classic ATS | What actually happens |

**The root cause:** JDs are keyword spam because no one knows which skills *actually* predict success.

### Core capability: Skill proximity, not keyword bingo

ATS models skills as binary checkboxes. Reality is a graph.

> ATS sees: JD needs "React" → CV has "Vue.js" → rejected.
>
> We see: Vue.js developer knows components, state management, hooks, virtual DOM. That's ~80% of React already. **1-2 weeks to ready.**

**Three layers of transferability:**

1. **Same-category transfer** — Vue.js ↔ React, Python ↔ JavaScript. Core concepts carry over; syntax is the only delta.
2. **Adjacent-domain transfer** — Excel → SQL. Different surface, same foundation: data manipulation, logical operations, structured thinking.
3. **Professional strength leverage** — A Sales person switching to a customer-facing tech role brings communication, objection handling, relationship management. These transfer directly.

**Result:** We don't ask "do you have this keyword?" We ask "what do you already know that's close?" Then we calculate the shortest path to readiness.

## 6. BUSINESS MODEL (STARTING POINT)

We go to market with **users first** — graduates and career pivoters — before monetizing employers. Once the candidate pool reaches critical mass, employer-side revenue activates.

### Tier 1: Free (acquisition engine)
Match score + gap overview + "you're X weeks away." No payment needed.

### Tier 2: One-time report — 49,000 VND
Full roadmap + course list + salary benchmarks for one target role. Cheaper than a bowl of phở — an easy yes after seeing the gap.

### Tier 3: Monthly subscription — 29,000 VND/mo
Progress tracking, re-assessment, multiple CV variants, unlimited role comparisons. Less than a cà phê sữa đá a week. Pivoters on a multi-month journey subscribe.

### Job placement affiliate (highest-margin)
After the roadmap, users see live job listings matched to their skill trajectory. When they apply and get hired via partner platforms (TopCV, etc.), Leveller earns a referral fee (~500k–2M VND per placement). This aligns incentives: we only win when the candidate wins.

> This is the **starting** income stream. Future streams include:
> - **Employer talent discovery** — Sourcing: employers pay to search the candidate pool by skill proximity
> - **JD refinement as a paid service** — Companies pay for smart JD builder + core vs trainable analysis
> - **API licensing** — Skill graph data, market benchmarks, gap analysis as an API for HR platforms

> - **Corporate L&D** — Internal mobility: skill gap analysis of existing workforce against future role needs
> - **Certification / skill verification** — Leveller becomes a trusted assessment layer; verified skill profiles
