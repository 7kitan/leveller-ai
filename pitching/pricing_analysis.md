# PRICING ANALYSIS

## The Model

| Tier | Price | What | User |
|------|-------|------|------|
| Free | First analysis free | Full roadmap, match score, courses, salary | Everyone |
| One-time | 59,000 VND | Full roadmap for one additional role | Casual users |
| Monthly | 199,000 VND/mo | Unlimited analyses, tracking, multiple roles | Career pivoters |
| Employer expansion | Enterprise | Talent discovery + smart JD builder | Companies |

---

## Why These Numbers

### Free: First analysis free
- **Before:** Free tier showed only a gap preview — user had to pay to see the actual roadmap.
- **Now:** User gets the full product once. They know exactly what they're buying.
- **AI cost:** ~$0.05/analysis. Burn is manageable and justified by higher conversion.

### One-off: 59,000 VND
- Intentionally **under 60K** — below a mental price threshold.
- More than phở (~50K), less than a cheap shirt (~100K). Feels like a small treat, not a purchase decision.
- At ~$2.40, 48x margin on a single analysis at $0.05 AI cost.

### Monthly: 199,000 VND
- Intentionally **under 200K** — below another round-number threshold.
- At ~$8/mo, comparable to a Netflix basic plan in Vietnam.
- Break-even vs one-off: ~3.4 analyses/month. Most career pivoters analyzing multiple roles will hit this naturally.
- The gap from one-off (59K) is wide enough that users don't feel nickled-and-dimed — but subscribers feel committed to getting value.

### Employer expansion: Enterprise
- Priced per-company, not per-user. Activated after the candidate pool reaches critical mass.
- This is the highest-margin tier and the long-term revenue driver.

## The User Flow

```
Land → Free analysis #1 (full product, no cost)
    ├── Got the answer → Leaves happy. You spent $0.05.
    ├── Needs one more → 59K one-off. Net ~$2.35.
    └── Planning a career change → 199K/mo subscriber.
        └── Stays 3-6 months → ~$24-48 net + data for the skill graph.
```

## Comparison with Previous Models

| Model | Free burn | Conversion | Net/100 users | Risk |
|-------|:---------:|:----------:|:-------------:|:----:|
| Original (gap preview + 49K + 29K) | ~$2 | Low | ~$15 | Low — safe but leaves money on table |
| First 3 free + 49K + 29K | ~$15 | Medium | ~$87 | Medium — $15 burn could be a loss |
| **First 1 free + 59K + 199K** | **~$5** | **Medium-high** | **~$85+** | **Low-medium — 3x less downside than 3-free** |

## Key Assumptions

1. **AI cost:** ~$0.05 per full analysis (GPT-4o-mini pipeline). Revisit if switching models.
2. **Conversion rate from free → paid:** Modeled at 15-25%. Needs validation.
3. **Subscriber retention:** Modeled at 3 months average. Career pivoters likely stay 2-6 months.
4. **Analyses per subscriber:** Modeled at 2-4/month. The product should incentivize frequent re-assessment (new job listings, progress tracking).

## Risk Mitigations

- **"One and done":** User gets their answer and leaves. Mitigated by the low free cost ($0.05) — this is a cheap acquisition funnel even at high churn.
- **Monthly too high:** 199K might deter students. Mitigated by the one-off option at 59K — they can always buy a single analysis.
- **Cannibalization:** Users buying 59K one-offs instead of subscribing. Mitigated by making subscription value obvious (progress tracking, re-assessment, multiple roles).
